Project Sekai: Colorful Stage

The game is set in Shibuya, Tokyo. A girl named Hoshino Ichika walks through the streets and she sees an LCD screen displaying Hatsune Miku. Upon returning home, she discovers a song named "Untitled" on her smartphone. Out of curiously, Ichika plays the songs, only to get surrounded by a white light and arrive at an unknown place. Hatsune Miku stands in front of Ichika, welcoming her to Sekai and inviting her to sing together.

5 reasons why this game is my favorite:

  • I like Project Sekai: Colorful Stage because of its fun and fair rhythm game mechanics where you do not lose if you miss one note.
  • I like Project Sekai: Colorful Stage because of the interesting story and relatable characters.
  • I like Project Sekai: Colorful Stage because of my favorite characters, Emu Otori and Toya Aoyagi.
  • I like Project Sekai: Colorful Stage because of my personal liking of Japanese and VOCALOID music.
  • I like Project Sekai: Colorful Stage because the 3D MVs inspire me to start learning how to dance again.
